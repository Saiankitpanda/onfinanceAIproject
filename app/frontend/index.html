ClauseMark AI

OCR, clause demarcation, and document Q&A in a Linux workbench UI.

Demo flow

  1. Upload a clean RBI or legal-style PDF with numbered clauses.
  2. Process the document and show extraction mode, blocks, and clauses.
  3. Open Clauses to show structured clause-level output.
  4. Open OCR to show raw text blocks used by the pipeline.
  5. Ask the agent for obligations, deadlines, penalties, or a short summary.
